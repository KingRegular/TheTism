This page is about how much I love different meals.

I'm sure you do too!

Meals are the most wholesome parts of the day.

They are just bundles of joy!

Let's go through them together, shall we?

Don't get left behind!

Breakfast

I love breakfast because I get a headstart away from the nightmares I experienced the previous night.

Lunch

Lunch is usually the middle of the day furthest away from the darkness. The safety doesn't last.

Dinner

Finally, dinner. They are ever so close. Run away while you can.

The day begins. You barely escaped.

There were millions of eyes that watched you as you slept. As the sun rose the eyes were seemilngly washed away. Were they a figment of your imagination? That couldn't be. AS long as you know they are gone for now. Wake up and put a smile on, it's time for breakfast one can only hope they don't show up again.

It's basically noon. They're still hiding.

You have gone the day without seeing any nightmares but be on your guard. You know the full extent of your horrors so you must understand why you have to always be alert. Don't let the apparitions get to you. It's easy imaging what may come to haunt you at night but please enjoy lunch for now.

Night time. You have to be ready.

The time has come again. He's waiting for you. There's no amount of words I can say to try and help you from what's on the other side of the darkness. Be not afraid, child. Just stomach down your dinner and make sure that you take a good look of all that's around you you never know if it'll be your last meal with him around. Enjoy dinner as much as you can.
